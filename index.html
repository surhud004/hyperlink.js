The world is full of emojis -

Look around! Technology is everywhere around you and in this fast-pacing world, if you are not tech-ready, you are out of this rat-race. It's all about texting, snapchatting, and a whole lot of different types of chatting. But, how do we express our emotions in messages? Enter Emojis. Emojis existed in messaging since email was invented. It's just that their UI has been updated. They went from :) to 🙂. And, in a society where textual communication is used casually and more frequently than face-to-face conversation, conveying emotion is as important as it is when speaking to someone in person. Recently, Google announced their new set of emojis coming to the Android keyboard update. (Did you just see Google's Twitter media without visiting the site? 😎)
